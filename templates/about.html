🌟 About Career Path Navigator

A student-focused platform to guide your career journey.

🎯 Our Mission

Career Path Navigator is designed to simplify career planning for students. We help learners discover suitable careers, explore skill-building courses, and connect with internship opportunities—all in one place.

📚 What We Offer

- A wide variety of career roadmaps across multiple industries.
- Links to free courses and resources from trusted platforms.
- A curated list of internship opportunities to gain real-world experience.
- A simple, intuitive design to make navigation easy for students.

🌍 Our Vision

To empower every student with the right tools, guidance, and resources to shape their future careers—regardless of their background or starting point.
